Your goal is to turn on all the light bulbs by pressing a subset of the buttons. Construct a CSP to solve this problem. Your CSP should have mmm﻿ variables and nnn﻿ constraints. For this problem only, you can use mmm﻿-ary constraints: constraints that can be functions of up to mmm﻿ variables. Describe your CSP precisely and concisely. from constraint import * # https://pypi.org/project/python-constraint/
problem = Problem()
problem.addVariable("a", [0,1]) # a: Button 1
problem.addVariable("b", [0,1]) # b: Button 2
f = lambda *buttons: sum(buttons)&1 and int(any(buttons)) 
problem.addConstraint(f, ("a"))
problem.addConstraint(f, ("a", "b"))
problem.addConstraint(f, ("b"))
problem.getSolutions() # []
